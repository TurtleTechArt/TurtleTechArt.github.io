Welcome to the first edition of The Shell!

  
  

The Turtle Tech Art Newsletter!

  

For the first ever edition of The Shell, we at Turtle Tech Art want to give an outline of who we are, why we’re starting this newsletter, and what to expect: 

  

- Who: Turtle Tech Art was founded in late 2024 by Mikyle Mosquera and Endlessette Osborn as a community for all things tech art. The newsletter is written by Henry Foley. We’re all current and former students!

  

- Why: We’re all passionate about this discipline and want to create a way to keep other TAs posted on happenings within the discipline. We hope you find this newsletter engaging and informative!

  

- What: This newsletter will contain updates from within the TA community, including but not limited to: News, Tutorials, Recent Projects, Events, and Job Openings. You can expect a new edition of The Shell on a monthly schedule

Seb van den Beemt‘s latest project is a toolkit allowing users to build and customize billboards for your scene. Users can procedurally create billboards using Houdini, edit their displays using Unreal’s toolkit, and render custom text using shaders.
