GDC Postmortem

  
  

Last month the Turtle Tech Art team attended the Game Developers Conference (GDC) in San Francisco. There we hosted the first ever TTA Meetup. We organized this event with the hope of getting 15-20 fellow tech artists to meet, but we were absolutely blown away by the support and attendance of over 100+! Thank you to everyone who attended and joined the community!

  
  

Updates to Productivity Jams

  
  

We're changing our Discord Productivity Jams up a bit. To make sure this is a place for you all to come and get some good work done - we've got a new format for you guys!

TWO spaces:
- Productivity Grind - A silent channel to work in peace together
- Productivity Chat - Where you can get some help or feedback on your projects! (A staff member will always be in here to help)

Join us every week if you’re looking for: A focused space to work, feedback on your latest project, or just a place to hang out and meet other tech artists!
